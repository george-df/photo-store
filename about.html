About

With an interest in technology and art, photography soon became a passion for this devoted Photographer. They began by shooting photos of family members, friends and nature, and soon developed their own personal style. Deciding on a career in photography in 2000 was the easiest choice they have ever made, and they’ve never looked back.

They use the best equipment available, and hold pre-shot consultations with clients to get a better understanding of their vision and preferences. If you’d like to learn more, please get in touch.
